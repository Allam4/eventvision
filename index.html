Our Story

We believe every celebration deserves to be beautiful

EventVision was born from a simple frustration: planning a meaningful event was unnecessarily complex, expensive, and exhausting. We set out to change that.

By combining the power of artificial intelligence with a community of passionate, talented vendors, we've created a platform where anyone — regardless of budget, time, or design expertise — can plan an event they'll be proud of.

We are entirely free for both users and vendors. Our mission is access — every birthday, wedding, and celebration deserves the same quality of thought and design.
